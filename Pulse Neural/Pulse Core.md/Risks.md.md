# Risks

Understanding risks early prevents major failure later.

---

## Product Risks

### Low User Density

- App becomes useless without enough users
    

### Weak Value

- If data is inaccurate, users won’t trust it
    

---

## Technical Risks

### Vibecoded Architecture

- Higher chance of bugs
    
- Harder to debug issues
    

### Performance Issues

- Animations lagging
    
- App breaking under load
    

---

## Growth Risks

### Launch Timing

- Launching when students are inactive
    

### Poor Retention

- Users try once and leave
    

---

## Business Risks

### Monetisation Difficulty

- Limited venues in small cities
    

### Easy to Copy

- Concept is simple to replicate
    

---

## Mitigation Strategies

- Focus on density before scaling
    
- Improve core loop before adding features
    
- Use AI (Claude) for debugging and system understanding
    
- Document everything clearly (this system)
    

---

## Key Insight

The biggest risk is not competition.

It is:

> Not reaching critical mass of users.
