# Core Metrics

These metrics determine whether Pulse is working.

---

## Primary Metric (North Star)

Weekly Active Users (WAU) who:

- Open the app before going out
    
- Interact with it (view or pulse)
    

---

## Supporting Metrics

### Engagement

- Pulses per night
    
- Sessions per user per week
    
- Time spent in app
    

---

### Retention

- % of users returning weekly
    
- % of users using app multiple nights
    

---

### Density (CRITICAL)

- Users active per night in a city
    
- Pulses per venue
    

---

## Early Targets

- 300–500 users in Galway
    
- Consistent weekly usage
    
- Noticeable differences between venues
    

---

## Leading Indicators

- Users checking app without prompting
    
- Organic sharing ("check Pulse")
    
- Group usage before nights out
    

---

## Warning Signs

- Users open once and don’t return
    
- All venues show similar activity
    
- Low pulse interaction
    

---

## Long-Term Metrics

- Revenue per user
    
- Venue engagement
    
- City expansion success
